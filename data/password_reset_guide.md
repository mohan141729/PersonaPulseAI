# Password Reset & Account Access Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to reset your password, recover access to your NexaCloud account, and resolve account lock issues.

---

## 1. Standard Password Reset

### Steps to Reset Your Password
1. Go to the NexaCloud login page: **https://app.nexacloud.io/login**
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"** below the login button
3. Enter your registered email address and click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your inbox (and spam/junk folder) for an email from `noreply@nexacloud.io`
5. Click the reset link within **30 minutes** — links expire after that
6. Create a new password meeting the requirements below
7. Click **"Update Password"** and log in with your new credentials

### Password Requirements
- Minimum **12 characters**
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 lowercase letter
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 special character (`!@#$%^&*`)
- Cannot reuse your last 5 passwords

---

## 2. Didn't Receive the Reset Email?

**Check the following:**
- Look in your **Spam / Junk** folder
- Ensure you're checking the correct email account
- Add `noreply@nexacloud.io` to your contacts/whitelist
- Wait 5 minutes — emails may be delayed during peak times

**Still not received?**
- Try the reset again from a different browser
- Contact your IT administrator if your company filters automated emails
- Open a support ticket at **https://support.nexacloud.io**

---

## 3. Account Locked

Accounts are locked after **5 consecutive failed login attempts** for security.

### Self-Service Unlock
1. Visit **https://app.nexacloud.io/unlock**
2. Enter your email address
3. Complete the identity verification challenge (email code + security question)
4. Your account will be unlocked immediately

### Admin Unlock (Organization Accounts)
If you are part of an organization, ask your **Organization Admin** to:
1. Navigate to **Admin Panel → Users → Active Users**
2. Search for your account
3. Click **"Unlock Account"**

### Unlock Not Working?
If self-service fails, contact support with:
- Your account email
- Your organization name
- The approximate time of the lockout

---

## 4. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Lost Access

If you lost access to your 2FA device:
1. On the login page, click **"Use Backup Code"**
2. Enter one of the 8-digit backup codes provided when you set up 2FA
3. After login, go to **Settings → Security** to reconfigure 2FA

### No Backup Codes?
Contact support at **security@nexacloud.io** with government-issued ID verification. Account recovery without 2FA may take **3–5 business days** due to security review.

---

## 5. SSO (Single Sign-On) Login Issues

For organizations using SSO (Google Workspace, Azure AD, Okta):
- Password reset must be done through your **Identity Provider**, not NexaCloud
- Contact your IT administrator if you cannot access your SSO provider
- NexaCloud admin can temporarily disable SSO requirement: **Admin → SSO Settings → Emergency Bypass**

---

## 6. Account Deletion & Reactivation

### Reactivating a Deactivated Account
Accounts deactivated within the last **30 days** can be reactivated:
1. Contact support@nexacloud.io with your account email
2. Provide business justification
3. Reactivation is processed within **1 business day**

Accounts deactivated for more than 30 days are permanently deleted per our data retention policy.
