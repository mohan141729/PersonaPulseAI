# Billing & Refund Policy

## Overview
NexaCloud is committed to transparent billing and fair refund policies. This document outlines how we charge, process refunds, and handle billing disputes.

---

## 1. Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price | Users | Storage | API Calls/Month |
|------|-------|-------|---------|-----------------|
| Starter | $29/month | Up to 5 | 10 GB | 50,000 |
| Professional | $99/month | Up to 25 | 100 GB | 500,000 |
| Business | $299/month | Up to 100 | 1 TB | 5,000,000 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

All plans are billed monthly or annually. **Annual plans receive a 20% discount.**

---

## 2. Billing Cycle

- Subscriptions renew automatically on your **billing anniversary date**
- You will receive an invoice email **7 days before** your renewal date
- Payment is processed on the **renewal date** using the card on file
- Invoices are available in **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**

### Prorated Charges
- **Upgrading mid-cycle:** You pay only the prorated difference for the remainder of the billing period
- **Downgrading mid-cycle:** Credit is applied to your next invoice
- **Adding users:** Prorated charge applied immediately

---

## 3. Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods:
- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- **ACH Bank Transfer** (US accounts only, for annual plans $1000+)
- **Wire Transfer** (Enterprise plans only)
- **PayPal** (available in select regions)

Payment details are managed in **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

---

## 4. Refund Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
New customers on the **Starter or Professional** plan are eligible for a full refund within **30 days of initial subscription**, no questions asked.

To request a refund:
1. Email **billing@nexacloud.io** with subject: "Refund Request – [Your Account Email]"
2. Include your account email and reason for cancellation
3. Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days**

### Prorated Refunds (After 30 Days)
- Monthly plans: No refunds after the 30-day window
- Annual plans: Eligible for a **prorated refund** of unused months if cancelled before the 6-month mark. After 6 months, no refunds are issued.

### Non-Refundable Items
- Add-on purchases (additional storage, premium support)
- Enterprise setup fees
- Usage overages already charged

---

## 5. Failed Payments

When a payment fails:
1. We retry **3 times** over 7 days
2. You receive an email notification after each failure
3. On the **3rd failure**, your account enters a **grace period** (7 additional days)
4. After the grace period, your account is **suspended** (data is retained for 30 days)
5. To reactivate, update your payment method in **Settings → Billing**

---

## 6. Billing Disputes

To dispute a charge:
1. Email **billing@nexacloud.io** within **60 days** of the charge
2. Include the invoice number and reason for dispute
3. Our billing team will respond within **2 business days**

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in account suspension.

---

## 7. Tax & VAT

- US customers: Sales tax applied based on your billing address
- EU customers: VAT applied at the applicable local rate. Provide your VAT number in **Settings → Billing → Tax Info** to have it included on invoices.
- Other regions: Prices are exclusive of local taxes; you are responsible for compliance

---

## 8. Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Your access continues until the end of the paid period
4. Data is retained for **30 days** after cancellation before permanent deletion
